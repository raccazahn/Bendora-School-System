# Code of Conduct — Bendora School System

<span style="color:#0056A3">**Bendora Technology is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for all contributors — regardless of background, identity, or location.**</span>

## 🌍 Our Pledge
In the interest of fostering an open and collaborative community, we pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, including:
- Students, teachers, and administrators in Liberia and beyond
- Engineers, designers, and QA specialists worldwide
- Contributors of all experience levels, genders, sexual orientations, disabilities, ethnicities, and beliefs

We believe that diverse perspectives build better educational technology — and that every voice matters.

## ✨ Expected Behavior
To help us achieve this, we ask all participants to:
- 🤝 **Be respectful and empathetic** in discussions, code reviews, and issue comments
- 🗣️ **Use inclusive language** (e.g., "they/them", avoid idioms that don't translate globally)
- 💡 **Welcome constructive feedback** — focus on ideas, not individuals
- 🌱 **Mentor and uplift** newer contributors, especially students and early-career developers in Liberia
- 🔐 **Respect privacy and data sensitivity** — never share real student/staff data in examples or issues
- 🇱🇷 **Honor local context** — consider infrastructure constraints (power, bandwidth) when proposing features

## 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior
Examples of unacceptable conduct include:
- Harassment, discrimination, or microaggressions of any kind
- Sexualized language, imagery, or unwelcome attention
- Trolling, insulting comments, or personal/political attacks
- Publishing others' private information (doxxing) without explicit permission
- Dismissing accessibility, low-bandwidth, or localization concerns as "edge cases"

## 🛡️ Reporting Guidelines
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:
1. **Document**: Save screenshots, message logs, or commit links (if safe to do so)
2. **Report**: Email `conduct@bendora.tech` with:
   - Your contact info (optional but helpful)
   - Description of the incident + context (issue/PR link, date, participants)
   - Any supporting evidence
3. **Response**: Our team will acknowledge within 48 hours and investigate confidentially

All reports are handled with care, confidentiality, and zero retaliation.

## 🔄 Enforcement & Consequences
Violations may result in:
- Private warning + education
- Temporary suspension from discussions/PR reviews
- Permanent ban from Bendora Technology projects (for severe/repeated violations)

Decisions are made by the Bendora Conduct Committee (`conduct@bendora.tech`) and are final.

## 🌱 Continuous Improvement
This Code of Conduct is a living document. We welcome suggestions to make it more inclusive, practical, or aligned with Liberian educational values. Open a Discussion or email us anytime.
